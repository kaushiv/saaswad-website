SHTE has a wide range of high quality oil coolers for the cooling of oil and hydraulic fluids. SHTE oil coolers are engineered to meet high specifications and technical requirements of our clients. SHTE offers an extensive range of nearly 50 oil coolers for application in a variety of products. SHTE is constantly expanding their oil cooler range to cover the demand in the market. Oil is without doubt one of the most crucial fluids contained within an engine, because it lubricates the moving parts of the engine and absorbs a lot of heat. Oil coolers are used to cool oil and hydraulic fluids for engines.. The oil cooler receives the lubricant’s heat and is cooled down by the ambient air or the radiator coolant. In some applications, the oil cooler is built into the radiator water tank. In modern vehicles, an automatic gearbox oil cooler is often designed as a stand-alone unit, mounted separately in the engine compartment or on the engine block.
