Our Products

Quality Products

Our Product lines include radiators such as industrial radiator & heavy duty radiator oil cooler such as hydraulic oil cooler & crane oil cooler, industrial heat exchangers and other heat transfer products. For markets like locomotives, power generation, construction, transportation, process manufacturing, mining and offshore exploration.

Saaswad Heat Transfer designs and manufactures these products with cost in mind, making sure they meet the performance and quality requirements of the application. For customers in this segment, SHTE balances manufacturing automation and labor, to drive a competitive cost point.
